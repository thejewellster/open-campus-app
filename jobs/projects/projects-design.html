• PROJECT, FINE ART // POSTED ON 6/18/2014
    Produce limited edition prints from original art using new digital technology
    Imagination Ink, Saratoga Springs, NY
    Continuous Work
    Volunteer with potential paid work in the future
    “Hi my name is Jim Olsen and I am the President of Imagination Ink. We are creating a start up that applies digital technology (e.g. variable data personalization, digital watermarks, interactive print, and augmented reality) to the art world in a way that hasn't been done before. As a startup, we're looking to economize in every way. This includes bringing together a group of five talented "starving artists" who are willing to invest their sweat equity by producing three pieces of art that will be considered to create a proof of concept for our future sales effort.”
