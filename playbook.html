Welcome to the Playbook by Open Campus

We're new, so let us tell you about what we're doing here.

Mission

Open Campus is an hyper-local, nationally scalable online community that connects students with jobs

Vision

Jobs, at the core, run the world. Your job, if not already, should be your lifestyle, something you love and something that inspires you. We make that happen for a demographic not yet exposed to the job world: students.

Upload

Students and businesses alike create profiles. Let the world know who you are and what you've been up to.

Post

Post a job, it's that easy. Select a specific skill set or "Student Professional."

Pinpoint

Pinpointing a student or a job takes the experience to a whole different level. If your visual, find one on our Map, or filter by whatever criteria seems good to you.

Hire

If you're hiring, tap a student and get connected. If you're looking to get hired. Apply with your profile, no lengthy forms needed.

Upload

Students upload a profile, which is condensed into a thumbnail, of everything they want and employer to know about them. Pull in social media pages and add all your experience and accomplishments. Build a visual resume. Show what you've done listing it on an 8.5x11 piece of paper.

Post

Posting a job is more an experience than anything. Watch your job post auto fill as you describe what type of student you're looking for. Post, it's only $25 bucks for hte next month months. Finding any type of employee should be enjoyable and easily accessible. That's why we bring the students to you. No more "post a job and let is float in cyber space" business.

Pinpoint

Find Pinpoint a job. Whether you're looking for a short music gig, or long term software engineering position, it's important that you can pinpoint exactly what you're looking for. With our filter features, you can do just that.

Pinpoint a Student

Unique to Open Campus, jobs find students. Students are more inclined to take a job when they are desired by the employer. Before we built Open Campus, we were handmatching students to jobs. 9/10 times students took the job when the employer was able to personally reach out to the student.
