Mission

Open Campus is an online community that connects college students looking for freelance jobs with local businesses who are seeking flexible, skilled and reliable help.

Vision

To create a new workforce of Student Professionals and personalize the world of online freelacning so students acheive their goal of getting real world experience and , and businesses achieve their goal of hiring talent within their community.
